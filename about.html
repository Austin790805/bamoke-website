About Us

Bamoke's Restaurant was founded in the year 2015 by Mr.Christopher Bamoke and it is run by the Bamoke family.Mr.christopher moved from Nairobi where the Restaurant used to be located previously and settled in Kitale due to capital issues. He then decided to start a small local hotel in 2015 and named it Bamoke Hotel.Due to it's local cuisine many customers loved it. Due to intensive income earning and requests from the customers he decided to now start building a restaurant in 2018.The restaurant was officially opened in 2020 and it was named The BAMOKE'S RESTAURANT. It has a total of 30 staff members ranging from the manager to the security details. The food here is prepared by qualified experienced chefs and served by eligible and swift waiters.

The restaurant has an outdoor and roof top space where customers can enjoy the cool breeze and the hot summer sun while taking their food.The inside has large glass walls where you can enjoy a clear view of the kilimani park on the side view and the quick mart mall on the front view.There is a large indoor and outdoor space with the state of the arch furnitures.The furnitures were precisely made to please the eye of the customers and make them as comfortable as possible since that is our major priority.The walls are hunged with several african art works representing our rich cultural heritage.The interior design is extremely tedious and exquisitely enticing.It was designed by one of the best interior designing company in the country and made precisely as our esteemed customers would love it.We strongly recommend this restaurant to any one who has been looking for a perfect place to satisfy his hunger.

What We Offer

We offer rich delicacy ranging from Hot chilling breakfast, tastey lunch, delicious and finger liking dinner to regular fast foods. The fast foods include French fries,McDonald's, Smoothie, KFC and so many more delicious foods. We also have a platform for ordering food online and the delivery is fast and free of charge.
